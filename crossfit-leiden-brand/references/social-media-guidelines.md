# CrossFit Leiden Social Media Guidelines

Guidelines for creating social media content that aligns with CrossFit Leiden's brand identity.

## Grid System and Safe Spaces

### Instagram Post (1:1 - 1080x1080px)
- **Grid**: 5 columns
- **Safe space margins**: 
  - Top: 100px
  - Bottom: 150px
  - Left/Right: 90px
- Keep critical text and logo within safe spaces

### Instagram Story / Reel (9:16 - 1080x1920px)
- **Grid**: Use vertical alignment
- **Safe space margins**:
  - Top: 150px (profile name area)
  - Bottom: 140px (interactive elements area)
  - Left/Right: Use column grid

### YouTube / Horizontal (16:9 - 1920x1080px)
- **Grid**: 9 columns
- **Safe space margins**:
  - Top/Bottom: 150px
  - Left/Right: 140px

## Visual Composition

### Color Usage
Follow the brand color proportions:
- **Cinnabar (#EF4C37)**: Largest areas, dominant presence
- **White/Black**: Secondary areas, text
- **Jonquil (#F7CB15)**: Accent elements
- **Verdigris/Chinese Violet**: Smaller accent touches

### Graphic Elements
Use the 45¬∞ diamond shapes from the brand identity:
- As background patterns
- Photo frames or masks
- Accent elements around text
- Dividers between content sections

**Important**: Keep shapes clean and geometric. They should feel intentional, not random.

### Logo Placement
- **1:1 Posts**: Bottom left corner within safe space
- **Stories**: Top left or bottom left, small
- **Cover images**: Top right or incorporated into design
- Use monochrome (black) on light backgrounds, inverted (white) on dark backgrounds

## Post Types and Templates

### 1. Testimonial Posts

**1:1 Format:**
- Large photo of the member (action shot or portrait)
- Overlay geometric shapes in brand colors
- Quote overlaid on image in white text
- Member name below quote
- Logo in bottom corner

**Story Format:**
- Full-screen photo with teal/coral geometric accents
- Quote in large text (Heading Now Medium)
- Member name
- Swipe-up/interactive elements at bottom

### 2. Campaign/Message Posts

**Key Visual Style:**
- Bold typography (Heading Now Medium for headlines)
- Large geometric shapes as design elements
- High contrast (Cinnabar on white, or white on dark photos)
- Clear hierarchy with Suisse Int'l for supporting text

**Example Headlines:**
- "YOUR ROAD TO FEELING GREAT"
- "EVERYDAY PEOPLE, EXCEPTIONALLY STRONG"

**Structure:**
- Main headline (large, impactful)
- Supporting text (optional, smaller)
- Call to action
- Logo and URL (crossfitleiden.com)

### 3. Trainer Introductions

**1:1 Format:**
- Professional but approachable photo
- Name and role
- Can include geometric shapes sparingly
- Introduction in caption, not on image

**Story Format:**
- Closer crop, personality-focused
- Minimal text overlay (just name if any)
- Story told in caption or text

### 4. Service/Program Announcements

**Visual Treatment:**
- Icon from brand iconography (6x6 grid style)
- Service name
- Brief description or CTA
- Background: Solid color (Jonquil, Cinnabar, or White) or photo with overlay

### 5. Event/Challenge Posts

**Approach:**
- Action photos from actual events
- Overlay brand graphics subtly
- Focus on community and energy
- Can be more dynamic with composition

## Story Highlights

### Categories and Covers
- **Classes**: Icon of group training, Verdigris background
- **Community**: Icon of team, Jonquil background
- **Events**: Icon of calendar, Cinnabar background
- **Coaches**: Icon or photo, neutral background
- **Results**: Before/afters or achievements

## Content Calendar

### Weekly Rhythm
- **Monday**: Motivation / Week kickoff
- **Tuesday**: Technique tip / Educational
- **Wednesday**: Community spotlight / Member feature
- **Thursday**: Event announcement / Program info
- **Friday**: Weekend challenge / Fun content
- **Saturday**: Event coverage / Action shots
- **Sunday**: Recovery / Lifestyle / Behind the scenes

### Monthly Themes
Rotate focus areas:
- Community stories
- Coach features
- Program highlights
- Event preparations
- Seasonal challenges

## Photography Guidelines

### Preferred Shots
- **Action**: Members mid-workout, genuine effort
- **Community**: Groups laughing, high-fiving, supporting
- **Coaching**: Coaches demonstrating or giving cues
- **Lifestyle**: Outdoor events, social gatherings
- **Facilities**: Clean, well-lit gym shots

### Photo Treatment
- Natural lighting preferred
- Authentic moments over posed
- Can add geometric overlays in brand colors
- Maintain high image quality
- Diverse representation of members

### What to Avoid
- Excessive filtering
- Over-posed shots
- Empty gym photos
- Poor lighting
- Cluttered backgrounds

## Video Content

### Formats
- **Reels**: 15-30 seconds, quick tip or highlight
- **Stories**: Behind the scenes, coach intros
- **Posts**: Longer form (up to 60s), workout demos or testimonials
- **YouTube**: Full-length content, event coverage

### Video Style
- Natural, authentic feel
- Can include music (upbeat but not aggressive)
- Overlay text with brand typography
- Include captions for accessibility
- End with CTA and logo

## Engagement Guidelines

### Responding to Comments
- Be prompt (within 24 hours)
- Use same friendly tone as posts
- Answer questions thoroughly
- Show appreciation for positive feedback
- Address concerns professionally

### Community Management
- Welcome new followers
- Share user-generated content (with permission)
- Celebrate member achievements
- Create conversation, not just broadcast

### Collaboration
- Tag members (with permission)
- Credit photographers
- Cross-promote with partner businesses
- Engage with local Leiden community
